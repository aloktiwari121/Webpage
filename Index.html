Summary & Vision

Highly motivated researcher specializing in computational modelling for lithium-ion batteries. Proven track record in DEM, CFD, and multi-scale modeling. Passionate about bridging particle science and next-generation energy technologies.

Key Projects

  • Understanding Calendering Behaviour in Lithium-ion Battery Electrodes

    Exploring compression behaviour via DEM and experiments, with applications in manufacturing optimization.

  • Calendering Temperature Effects

    Coupled DEM and heat transfer modeling to study microstructure and electrochemical performance.

  • Granular Bed Vibration Studies

    Investigated granular temperature, stresses, and mixing behaviour in vibrated beds using DEM.
